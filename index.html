Publisher

Multi-platform video publishing tool

A private publishing workflow for uploading, scheduling, and posting short-form videos to authorized social media accounts.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

App Info Terms of Service Privacy Policy

What this app does

Publisher helps authorized operators prepare one video, add a title, description, hashtags, and a scheduled publish time, then publish that video to selected social platforms.

支援的工作流程包含影片上傳、標題描述與 hashtags 編輯、排程時間設定，以及發布到已授權的社群帳號。

TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API

The app uses TikTok Login Kit so a TikTok account owner can authorize the app. After authorization, the app uses TikTok's Content Posting API only to publish videos selected by the authorized user.

本工具會透過 TikTok Login Kit 取得使用者授權，並且只會用於使用者明確選擇的影片發布流程。

Account access

  • The app does not ask for TikTok passwords.
  • The app stores access tokens only for authorized publishing workflows.
  • The app does not sell personal data.
  • Users can revoke app access from their TikTok account settings.
