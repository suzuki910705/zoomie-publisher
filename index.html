What this app does

Publisher is a web app for managing a repeatable short-form video publishing workflow. It is designed for authorized operators who manage their own connected creator accounts.

Upload and prepare

Upload one video, enter a shared title, description, hashtags, and select the destination platforms for that post.

Schedule posts

Choose an exact publish time or use built-in morning and evening scheduling shortcuts. Scheduled tasks are processed by the Publisher server.

Platform mapping

Publisher maps the same post data to each platform's required fields, such as YouTube title/tags and TikTok post text.

Account checks

Before posting to TikTok, Publisher verifies the authorized creator username to reduce the risk of publishing to the wrong account.

TikTok integration

Publisher uses TikTok for Developers capabilities only after the TikTok account owner authorizes the app. TikTok data is used for account verification and selected video publishing.

Products and scopes used

  • Login Kit: lets the TikTok account owner authorize Publisher through OAuth.
  • user.info.basic: queries creator information and privacy options, including the creator username used for account verification.
  • video.publish: publishes videos selected or scheduled by the authorized operator through the Content Posting API.

Publishing workflow

  1. 1

    The operator connects an authorized creator account through the platform OAuth flow.

  2. 2

    The operator uploads a video and enters title, description, hashtags, and schedule time.

  3. 3

    Publisher verifies the configured destination account before sending content to platform APIs.

  4. 4

    At the scheduled time, Publisher uploads the selected video and post metadata to the chosen destinations.

Data and control

Publisher does not ask for TikTok passwords. OAuth tokens are used only for authorized publishing workflows. Operators can revoke TikTok app access from their TikTok account settings.
