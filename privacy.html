Privacy Policy

This policy explains how ZoomieAI Publisher handles account authorization data and publishing content.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

App Info Terms of Service Privacy Policy

1. Information we collect

The app may process the following information when an authorized user uses the publishing workflow:

  • Uploaded video files selected by the user.
  • Video title, description, hashtags, schedule time, and platform choices.
  • OAuth access tokens or refresh tokens for connected platforms.
  • Basic creator or account information returned by platform APIs, such as TikTok creator username and privacy options.
  • Publishing status, API responses, error messages, and timestamps.

2. How we use information

We use this information only to authenticate authorized accounts, prepare publishing metadata, upload selected videos, schedule posts, publish content, and troubleshoot publishing failures.

本工具只會將資料用於授權、排程、發布與錯誤排查，不會販售個人資料。

3. TikTok data

When you authorize TikTok access, the app uses TikTok Login Kit to obtain an access token. The app may use that token to query creator info and publish videos selected by the authorized user through TikTok's Content Posting API.

The app does not collect TikTok passwords and does not post videos unless a user creates, schedules, or manually triggers a publishing task.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared with social platforms only when needed to complete the publishing actions requested by the authorized user.

5. Storage and security

Publishing data and credentials may be stored in the app environment or configuration files used by the operator. The operator is responsible for securing server access, credential files, environment variables, and backups.

6. Retention

Publishing records and uploaded media are retained only as long as needed for the publishing workflow, audit, troubleshooting, or operational needs. The operator may delete local records and uploaded files when no longer required.

7. Revoking access

You can revoke TikTok app access in your TikTok account settings. You can also contact the operator of the website where this page is hosted to request removal of stored credentials or publishing records.

8. Children's privacy

This app is intended for authorized operators managing social media publishing workflows. It is not intended for children.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page indicates the latest revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the operator of the website where this page is hosted.
